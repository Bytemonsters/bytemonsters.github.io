What are Bytemons?

Bytemons is a non-fungible token (NFT) Collectible and Trading Card where each Bytemon, represents a creature, within the Bytemon world.

Each Bytemon is unique, carefully hand crafted from start to finish, also they are animated and stored as a token on the Ethereum blockchain.

Why get a Bytemon NFT?

NFT's allow anyone to start a digital collection right now think NFT's as one investment, one investment in buying, selling and trading the collections.

This is changing the way we collect, buy and trade digital assets. We want cardholders to collect all of them, trade, sell, make friends and to be proud to have all the cards. It is time to join the NFT world!

We want you to have fun collecting them, and Bytemons are cool creatures living in this world waiting for you to start an awesome collection!!

Do not miss any drop because there will be a limited supply of cards, and the pricing follows a curve to allow early adopters to collect.

The Release Curve (drop)

The purchase price will increase over time following a curve so that more people can have a chance to get one for lower prices.
This way we can promote collecting, create hype, reward early supporters, trading and build a community. Fair prices starting from 0.01 ETH and most of them are under 0.2 ETH and Legendary at max. 1 ETH.

Collectible Bytemons

Each Bytemon will be featured as an artistically inspired collectible card that is tied to a token on the Ethereum blockchain, which will allow owners to trade and make digital sales of each item using cryptocurrency. Each of these NFTs will have a limited card number and designed to fit the Bytemon world.

We will be releasing four tiers of cards (Evolution level 1 to 3 and Legendary cards).
Cards rarity will range from few starting levels to Legendary levels. 150 unique animated cards and 15 Legendary types.
